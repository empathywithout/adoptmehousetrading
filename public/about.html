About Us

A platform built by Adopt Me house traders, for Adopt Me house traders.

What this is

AdoptMeHouseTrading.com is a community-built platform dedicated to Adopt Me house trading. We started because there was nowhere to properly list a house for trade, no way to look up what a house was actually worth, and no system for verifying who built something first.

So we built one. This site lets you browse live house listings, post your own, find a builder for a custom commission, check real trade comps, and register your build so there is a permanent record of who made it.

Real values from real trades

Most value lists in the Adopt Me space are just one team's opinion. We are taking a different approach. Every value on this site is built from actual verified completed trades, submitted by real players with proof. The more trades that get recorded, the more accurate the data gets. We are building this from the ground up with the community, not behind closed doors.

Want to help? The Data Team is open to applications.

The Build Registry

Houses get cloned constantly. The Build Registry exists to solve that. When you register a build, you are creating a timestamped record of your original work. Anyone can look up a house and see who built it first, when they built it, and what it looks like. It is not a perfect system but it is a lot better than nothing.

Community first

This site does not hold your items, guarantee trades, or act as an escrow. We are a matchmaker. The actual trade always happens between two real players in-game. What we provide is the infrastructure to find each other, verify listings, and settle disputes if something goes wrong.

We have an active Discord community where traders hang out, share builds, and help each other out. Come join us.
