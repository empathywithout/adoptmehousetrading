About Us

A trading platform built by the Adopt Me community, for the Adopt Me community.

What is AdoptMeHouseTrading.com?

AdoptMeHouseTrading.com is a fan-made platform dedicated to Adopt Me house trading. We built it because house trading had no real home online. There was no good place to list your house, find serious offers, verify who built a build, or look up what houses are actually worth based on real trades.

This site is that place. You can list your house, make offers, commission a builder, browse the build registry, and see what similar houses have actually traded for. Everything is designed around making trades safer, faster, and more fair.

Real values, not guesses

Most value lists in the Adopt Me space are based on one person's or one team's opinion. We are doing it differently. Our house and pet values are built from real verified completed trades submitted by our community data team. The more trades get logged, the more accurate the values become for everyone.

We are still building this out, and if you trade regularly and want to help, you can apply to join the data team.

The Build Registry

The build registry is one of our most unique features. Builders can register their original builds with timestamped proof, creating a permanent record of who built what and when. It helps protect original creators and gives traders a way to verify a build's history before committing to a trade.
